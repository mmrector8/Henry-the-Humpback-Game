Henry, the Humpback Whale Game

Instructions

In the spring and summer in Monterey Bay, CA, USA:

Humpback whales need to eat around 3000 lbs of krill a day to prepare for their migration south to Mexico.

Your job is to help Henry, a young humpback whale, eat 3000 lbs of krill and avoid obstacles in his path.

Use the keyboard arrows to control Henry as he swims and make sure to keep track of Henry's health level.

He will gain health by eating and lose health by colliding with obstacles.

Kelp and crabs will not hurt Henry!
